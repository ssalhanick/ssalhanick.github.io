By Car

Japanese Garden
at the Fort Worth Botanic Garden
3300 Japanese Garden Lane
Fort Worth, TX 76107

The Fort Worth Botanic Gardens are located a mile West of Downtown Fort Worth. Exit I-30 W at S University Drive and turn right on University Drive. The Gardens will be on your left. Follow the signs to the Japanese Garden and we'll see you there!
